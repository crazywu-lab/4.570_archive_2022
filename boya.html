Fantasy to Illustration
-Visualization of Fantasy Geographic Landscape in Borges’s Fictions-

Boya Zhou

Final project for the MIT class 4.550/4.570 Computation Design Lab
Development: February-May, 2022
Instructor: Prof. Takehiko Nagakura, Dr. Daniel Tsai & Prof. Guzden Varinlioglu

Overview

Borges was born in Argentina, while his work includes detailed imagination of places all over the world.
His writing map is actually the map of his reading history – his fantasy landscape is based on some geographic references and some fabrication, which like a random mixture.

Project Overview

Both artificial while designed by human consciousness, which is influenced by both personal experience and cultural context. Can we design a new kind of landscape from the inspiration of text? Can AI go beyond personal bias?
Why illustration? Easier to implement than models. Have the potential to combine ‘illogical’ and ‘unrelated’ elements together.
